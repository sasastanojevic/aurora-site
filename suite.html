FAQ

Suite-specific questions.

Is the Suite really cheaper than buying RF Agency + CP Agency separately?

Yes — $999/mo vs. $599 + $799 = $1,398 stacked. But the actual reason the Suite exists is the signal pipeline, not the discount. ReForge publishes seven content events into ClientPulse as a privileged signal. Content velocity per client becomes a leading churn indicator no standalone CS product can offer. That's the feature you're paying for. The bundle math is a side effect.

When does the signal pipeline activate?

Launch+30 to launch+45. Both products go live on launch day; the pipeline (RF events streaming into CP via the privileged connector) switches on once the first cohort of Suite customers has enough content history for the signals to stabilise. You'll be notified the day it activates on your account.

What if we only need ReForge or only ClientPulse?

Then don't buy the Suite. Both products work standalone. ReForge Agency is $599/mo, ClientPulse Agency is $799/mo, and you can see the full pricing table on the main pricing page. The Suite is specifically for agencies that need both halves of the data — and want the content-velocity signal feeding their retention dashboard.

Can we add more seats?
