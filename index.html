FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need both products?

No. ReForge and ClientPulse work independently. The Agency Suite is the only place they talk to each other: ReForge publishes content events (velocity, channel diversity, voice freshness), ClientPulse consumes them as a privileged signal, and content velocity becomes a leading churn indicator no standalone CS platform can offer. The Suite is a feature, not a bundle discount.

How is this different from ChatGPT, Jasper, or HubSpot?

ChatGPT can't connect to your Stripe, hold a separate voice profile per client, or learn from approval/rejection events. Jasper has Brand IQ and an MCP server — but MCP is gated to their Business tier ($300+/seat). HubSpot Marketing Pro has MCP at $890/mo plus $3,000 onboarding. Aurora is the only sub-$1K MCP-native bundle for a 5–15 person agency, with the closed-loop voice learning none of them ship.

What's the Solo+ tier?

Solo $49 ships with GPT-4o-mini and no recursive voice learning — the kicking-the-tires plan. Solo+ at $99/mo unlocks all models (Claude, GPT, Gemini), recursive voice learning that climbs first-draft approval rates from 40% to 80%+ over 90 days, and 3 MCP connections. Same single-seat / single-voice scope as Solo. The right plan if you're already paying a ghostwriter $1,200+ per month.

What channels are supported at launch?

YouTube (direct API), X/Twitter (direct API, pay-per-use), Apple Podcasts (Connect API). LinkedIn ships via email-bridge (forwarded notification emails parsed) at launch; native API switches on once our Community Management API approval lands. Meta (Facebook Pages, Instagram Business) follows post-HRB. Spotify Podcasters and Substack don't expose APIs we can use yet. Honest about native vs. bridge so you know which has full fidelity.

Is my data safe?

All data is stored in EU Frankfurt (Supabase), encrypted at rest and in transit. Your data never trains our AI models. GDPR compliant with full data retention controls and account deletion.
