FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need both products?

No. ReForge and ClientPulse work independently. But agencies using both get the full revenue lifecycle — win clients with content, keep them with intelligence. The Agency Suite bundle saves $399/month ($599 + $799 stacked = $1,398, bundle is $999).

How is this different from ChatGPT or Jasper?

ChatGPT can't connect to your Stripe account, maintain voice profiles across 20 clients, or send you a Monday morning email with churn alerts. Aurora products are persistent intelligence systems, not prompt wrappers.

What integrations are supported?

ReForge publishes to X/Twitter, LinkedIn, and WordPress (more coming). ClientPulse connects to Stripe, Google Calendar, Gmail, and Zoom. Both use OAuth — no API keys needed.

Is my data safe?

All data is stored in EU Frankfurt (Supabase), encrypted at rest and in transit. Your data never trains our AI models. We're GDPR compliant with full data retention controls and account deletion.

Plans start at $49/mo for ReForge (Solo) and $59/mo for ClientPulse (Solo). No free tier. Cancel anytime. Early Adopters get 30% off the first year on Agency + Agency Suite — redeem at Stripe Checkout with EA-RF-AGENCY-30, EA-CP-AGENCY-30, or EA-SUITE-30 (20 slots each).

Absolutely. If you manage 5+ clients and need leverage, Aurora works for you. The Solo tiers are designed specifically for solo operators.

Those are prompt-and-generate tools — great for one-off drafts. It replaces the "content assembly line," not just the first draft. Most teams find they cancel the other tool within a month.

Most agencies see value on Day 1. Drop a single podcast into ReForge and get 50+ ready-to-publish pieces in under 10 minutes. Connect Stripe to ClientPulse and see your client health scores instantly. The "aha" moment isn't weeks away — it's your first session.
